In Development Launch: Summer 2026

iOS App — v1.0

The core platform, built for serious cyclists. Adaptive training load, intelligent recovery, and structured workouts pushed straight to your devices.

📈
Adaptive Training Load
CTL, ATL & TSS tracked in real time. Plans adjust automatically.
⚡
FTP & Power Curve
Continuous eFTP estimation via CP2 model. No manual tests needed.
🫀
HRV-Driven Recovery
Reads HRV, resting HR & sleep from Apple Health. Adjusts intensity daily.
🔁
Push Workouts
Structured sessions to Garmin, Zwift & MyWhoosh via intervals.icu.
🔗
Strava & Garmin Sync
Imports rides automatically. No manual uploads.
📅
Plan Cascading
Skipped sessions trigger automatic schedule adjustments.
